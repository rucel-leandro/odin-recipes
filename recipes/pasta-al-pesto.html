Pasta al Pesto

A Genovese classic with an insanly quick cooking time

Ingredients

  • 80 grams of pasta
  • Salt
  • Boiling Water
  • 30 grams of pesto alla Genovese
  • Extra vergin olive oil
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Put the water to boil
  2. Add salt to the water
  3. Add the pasta to the boling water
  4. Leave the pasta boil until it is soft (about 8 - 10 minutes)
  5. Filter the water out of the pasta
  6. Add extra vergin olive oil
  7. Add Pesto
  8. Add basil leaves to the pasta
